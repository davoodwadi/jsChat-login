Revolutionize Your Conversations with Spreed

A major flaw of the current LLM chatbots is their linear nature. You have multiple questions about the same response? You must ask them linearly. By the time your first question is answered you have follow-up questions on the new response. There’s also no place to ask a second question on the first response. When the conversation is over, you end up with a long linear chat history. It’s hard to retrieve information. It’s also hard to organize your thoughts and find what’s where. Spreed gets rid of this limitation.

With Spreed, when you ask a question and get a response, you can ask multiple follow-up questions on the same response. Each question will branch out into its own dialogue path, maintaining the relevant LLM chat memory for each branch. You end up with alternate realities that are isolated from its neighboring questions. You can also modify LLM responses directly in the UI, so if the chatbot makes a mistake, you can just give direct comment to it in the assistant box, or even modify the parts of the response you don't like.

When you’re done, you’ll be left with an organized and accessible mind map that you can browse, review, or expand upon at any time.
